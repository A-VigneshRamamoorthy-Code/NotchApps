# Notch Apps

**Playful little apps that live right inside your MacBook notch.**

👉 **[notchapps.app](https://a-vigneshramamoorthy-code.github.io/NotchApps/)**

---

🐾 **NotchPaw** — A springy creature pounces from your notch to catch your cursor. 8 critters, real physics, zero CPU when idle.

🔒 **NotchLock** — A pull-cord dangles from your notch. Tug it to lock your Mac. 4 cord styles.

Both apps are **free**, **open-source**, and built natively for macOS.
